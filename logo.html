A logo design is a visual symbol that represents a brand, organization, or group. It combines typography, imagery, and colors to create a unique and recognizable identity. A well-designed logo should be simple, memorable, versatile, and relevant to its purpose.

Types of Logos:

  • Wordmark (Logotype) – A text-based logo using custom typography (e.g., Coca-Cola).
  • Lettermark (Monogram) – Initial-based logos for simplicity (e.g., NASA).
  • Icon (Symbol or Emblem) – A graphical representation (e.g., Apple).
  • Combination Mark – A mix of text and symbol (e.g., Adidas).
  • Emblem – A traditional, badge-like design (e.g., Harley-Davidson).
