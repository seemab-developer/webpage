Home Page Logo Poster

A poster is a visual communication tool used to convey a message, promote an event, or raise awareness about a cause. It combines images, typography, and colors in an eye-catching layout to attract attention and engage viewers.

Types of Posters:

  • Promotional Posters – Used for advertising products, movies, or events.
  • Propaganda Posters – Designed to influence public opinion and inspire action.
  • Informational Posters – Used for educational or awareness campaigns.
  • Artistic Posters – Focused on creative expression and aesthetics.

Since you’re working on a resistance group with a propaganda goal and a freedom theme, your poster should feature bold imagery, strong slogans, and symbolic elements like raised fists, broken chains, or silhouettes of revolutionaries. High-contrast colors like red, black, and white can evoke power and urgency.
